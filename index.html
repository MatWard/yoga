Welcome to the amazing world of Yoga

I have been teaching yoga since 1979 having trained with the British Wheel of Yoga and Friends of Yoga International. It has been a real joy to teach people of all ages to a wide variety of groups. That is the amazing thing about Yoga - it can be practised and enjoyed no matter what your age, background or ability.

The word Yoga means Union (yoke) and is an ancient discipline.

Hatha yoga combines

  • Movement (asana)
  • Breath control (pranayama)
  • Concentration
  • Deep relaxation

With regular practice it brings suppleness, strength and stamina to the body. At the same time it concentrates, relaxes and stills the mind.

All you need is the will to give it a try, a small mat or rug to work on, and a little space to stretch...you will be amazed.
